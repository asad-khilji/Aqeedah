Aqeedah

Where do we take our Aqeedah from?

The Quran and Sunnah

Where is Allah?

Above the heavens and over the throne

Where is the evidence that Allah is over the throne?

Surah Taha 20:5 ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنُ عَلَى ٱلْعَرْشِ ٱسْتَوَىٰ

What is the meaning of istawa?

To rise over and to ascend

Why did Allah create Jinn and Mankind?

To worship Allah alone and not associating partners with Allah

Where is the evidence that Allah created us to worship Him?

Quran 51:56 وَمَا خَلَقْتُ ٱلْجِنَّ وَٱلْإِنسَ إِلَّا لِيَعْبُدُونِ

What is the meaning of they worship?

To make one

What is the meaning of la ilaha ila Allah?

There is no one worthy of worship except Allah

What is the greatest act of worship?

Tawheed

What is Tawheed?

Singling out Allah in worship

What is the greatest act of disobedience?

Shirk

What is shirk?

Worshipping other than Allah and along with Allah

How many categories of Tawheed are there?

3

What are the three categories of Tawheed?

Tawheed Ruboobiyyah, Tawheed Uloohiyyah, and Tawheed Al Asma wal Sifat

What is Tawheed Ruboobiyyah?

Singling out Allah in regards to His actions such as Creating and Providing

What is the meaning of Tawheed Uloohiyyah?

Singling out Allah in regards to the actions of His slaves such as dua, slaughter, and sujood

Are there names and attributes that beling to Allah?

Yes

From where do we get the names of Allah?

Quran and Sunnah

Are the attributes of Allah similar to our attributes?

No

Where is the evidence that the attributes of Allah are unique?

Surah Shura 42:11 فَاطِرُ ٱلسَّمَـٰوَٰتِ وَٱلْأَرْضِ ۚ جَعَلَ لَكُم مِّنْ أَنفُسِكُمْ أَزْوَٰجًۭا وَمِنَ ٱلْأَنْعَـٰمِ أَزْوَٰجًۭا ۖ يَذْرَؤُكُمْ فِيهِ ۚ لَيْسَ كَمِثْلِهِۦ شَىْءٌۭ ۖ وَهُوَ ٱلسَّمِيعُ ٱلْبَصِيرُ
